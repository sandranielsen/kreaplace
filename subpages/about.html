Our Mission

We want you to feel feel like you are apart of something more - a community.

KreaPlace is a platform that gathers anything related to knitting and crocheting into one place, making it easy for enthusiasts like you and me to find exactly what we are looking for. Whether it is patterns, inspiration or advice from fellow knitting and crocheting interested people - the possibilities are endless.

The concept behind KreaPlace arose when I, Joan, started crocheting in 2015 and I was missing a place, where I easily could find patterns. I was not able to find a platform, that met all of my needs. So I decided to take matters into my own hands and thus KreaPlace was created.
